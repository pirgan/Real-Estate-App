# About Meridian Realty Group

## Our Story

Meridian Realty Group was founded in 2005 by James Hartley and Rebecca Blake with a single office in Chelsea. Today we operate from 9 offices across London with a team of over 120 agents, negotiators, and property managers. We have sold and let more than 14,000 properties and manage a rental portfolio of over 800 homes.

Our mission is simple: **to provide the most trusted, transparent, and technology-forward property service in London**.

## What Makes Us Different

### AI-Powered Listings
Every property listed with Meridian benefits from our proprietary AI tools:
- **AI Description Generator** — creates compelling, accurate listing copy in seconds
- **AI Valuation Insights** — benchmarks price against comparable sales and market trends
- **Photo Analyser** — identifies rooms, condition, and key features from uploaded images
- **Natural Language Search** — buyers describe their ideal home in plain English

### Human Expertise
Technology augments but does not replace our people. Every Meridian agent is NAEA Propertymark qualified with a minimum of three years' experience. We invest heavily in continuous training and local market knowledge.

### Transparency
- No hidden fees — our selling fee is always 1.2% + VAT, published upfront
- No pressure selling — we never cold-call
- Real-time portal updates — sellers and landlords see every viewing and offer as it happens
