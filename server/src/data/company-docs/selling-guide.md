# Selling Your Property with Meridian Realty Group

## Why Choose Meridian?

Meridian Realty Group has sold over 4,000 properties across London and the South East since 2005. Our average time to sell is 38 days — 22% faster than the industry average. We combine professional photography, AI-enhanced listings, and targeted digital marketing to achieve the best price for your home.

## Step 1 — Valuation

Request a free, no-obligation market appraisal. Our valuation experts analyse:
- Recent comparable sales within a 0.5-mile radius
- Current demand and buyer activity for your property type
- Condition, presentation, and potential improvement areas

We provide a realistic asking price range rather than inflated estimates.

## Step 2 — Instruction and Preparation

Once you instruct Meridian, we:
- Arrange professional photography and floor plan measurement
- Write an AI-enhanced listing description highlighting key selling points
- Obtain your Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) — legally required before listing

Prepare your property by decluttering, touching up paintwork, and ensuring the exterior is tidy. First impressions matter enormously online.

## Step 3 — Marketing Launch

Your property goes live on Meridian's portal, Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket simultaneously. We also market to our database of registered buyers who match your property profile.

## Step 4 — Viewings and Offers

Our agents conduct accompanied viewings and provide feedback after every visit. We negotiate on your behalf to achieve the best possible price and terms. All offers are presented promptly with the buyer's financial position assessed.

## Step 5 — Sale Agreed

Once you accept an offer:
1. Instruct your solicitor to prepare the draft contract
2. Complete the Property Information Form (TA6) and Fittings and Contents Form (TA10)
3. Order your EPC if not already done

## Step 6 — Conveyancing to Completion

Your solicitor will handle enquiries from the buyer's solicitor. Common enquiries relate to boundaries, planning permissions, and building regulations. Respond promptly to avoid delays. Exchange of contracts typically occurs 8–12 weeks after offer acceptance.

## Fees

Meridian's selling fee is 1.2% + VAT of the achieved sale price (no sale, no fee). This includes all marketing, photography, accompanied viewings, negotiation, and progression support.
