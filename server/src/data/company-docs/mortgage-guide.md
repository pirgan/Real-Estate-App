# Mortgage Guide — Meridian Realty Group

## Understanding Mortgages

A mortgage is a loan secured against your property. If you fail to keep up repayments, the lender can repossess your home. It is therefore essential to borrow within your means and understand the product you are taking on.

## Types of Mortgage

### Repayment Mortgage
Each monthly payment reduces both the interest and the capital balance. By the end of the term (typically 25–35 years) the mortgage is fully repaid. This is the most common type for residential buyers.

### Interest-Only Mortgage
Monthly payments cover only the interest. The capital must be repaid in full at the end of the term via a separate repayment vehicle (e.g. ISA, investment portfolio). Mainly used by buy-to-let investors.

## Fixed vs Variable Rates

| Type | Description | Best for |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Fixed | Rate locked for 2, 5, or 10 years | Certainty and budgeting |
| Tracker | Follows Bank of England base rate | When rates are falling |
| Discount | Set % below lender's standard variable rate | Short-term savings |
| SVR | Lender's default rate after initial deal ends | Always remortgage before reaching SVR |

## How Much Can You Borrow?

Lenders typically offer 4–4.5× your annual income. With a joint application, they use combined income. Your credit score, existing debts, and monthly outgoings all affect the final offer.

**Example**: Combined income of £80,000 → mortgage of up to £360,000.

## Deposit Requirements

| Deposit % | LTV | Typical rate availability |
|-----------|-----|--------------------------|
| 5% | 95% | Limited, higher rates |
| 10% | 90% | Good range of products |
| 15% | 85% | Better rates |
| 25%+ | 75% | Best rates available |

## The Mortgage Application Process

1. **Agreement in Principle (AIP)** — soft credit check, takes 15 minutes online
2. **Full application** — detailed income, expenditure, and employment verification
3. **Valuation** — lender instructs a surveyor to confirm the property is worth the loan
4. **Mortgage offer** — issued within 2–6 weeks of full application
5. **Completion** — funds released to solicitor on completion day

## Remortgaging

When your initial deal ends, switch to a new deal rather than falling onto the SVR. Start the remortgage process 3–6 months before your deal expires. Switching lenders can save thousands of pounds per year.

## Buy-to-Let Mortgages

- Minimum deposit: 25%
- Stress test: rental income must cover 125–145% of monthly mortgage payment
- Interest-only common; rental income taxed as income (allowable expenses deductible)
